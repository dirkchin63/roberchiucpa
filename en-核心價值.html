Core Value & Professional Qualifications

Core Value - More than just company formation

Profit Accounting Company Limited were established in 2009. We provide services of company formation, company secretarial, tax filing, accounting and trademark registration to support beginners as well as small and medium size enterprises (SME) in starting up their own businesses and most importantly, the annual compliance maintenance required by Law. 

Compared with Business Centres, consulting companies and secretarial companies in the market, we do not need to outsource client's tax filling and accounting services to others. As a result, we can help you save money and time.

Our mission is to provide you with company registration, annual compliance of accounting and tax filing, everything your company requires all under one roof of Profit Accounting Company Limited without having the need to go elsewhere, therefore enabling you to concentrate on your clients and building revenue. We position ourselves as the bridge between our clients, the Hong Kong Inland Revenue Department and the Hong Kong Companies Registry. 

Throughout years of effort, we have built up our credibility in the industry and our customer-oriented professional service has consolidated our role as a Reliable Accountant in the region. We believe that reliability is based upon professionalism therefore, our core value is to provide honest, reliable and professional services to our clients.

For service details and fee schedules, please click the separate titles on the above.

 

Professional Qualifications 

PROFIT ACCOUNTING COMPANY LIMITED is a Trust and Company Service Provider licenced by the Hong Kong Companies Registry. Please refer to the "Register of Trust or Company Service Provider Licensees". We are professional and reliable company registration agent. Our staff have a solid foundation in accounting and taxation knowledge and variety of experience in the field. 

Since 2005, The Hong Kong Institute of the Certified Public Accountants (HKICPA) pointed out that complaints have been received by unknowing customers, who engaged the services of business centres, consulting companies, secretarial companies and unlicensed accounting firms. They often end up wasting more time, effort and money.

For further details, please refer to the report from The Hong Kong Institute of the Certified Public Accountants. 

The cornerstone of our services is our Professional Qualifications and experience.
